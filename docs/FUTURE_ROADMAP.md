## 🧪 Innovation Ideas

### 1. AI-Powered Journaling

- Smart journal with daily prompts
- AI analyzes entries for mood patterns
- Integrates with mood tracker

### 2. Virtual Safe Space

- 3D/VR calming environment
- Guided meditation in virtual nature
- Ambient sound therapy

### 3. Academic-Wellness Correlation

- Link academic calendar with stress data
- Identify high-risk academic periods
- Suggest preemptive interventions

### 4. Sleep Intelligence

- Sleep quality tracking
- Correlation with mental health scores
- Sleep hygiene recommendations

### 5. Social Connection Engine

- Match students with similar experiences
- Facilitate anonymous buddy system
- Study group recommendations

---

## 📈 Growth Strategy

### University Partnerships

1. Pilot with 1-2 universities
2. Collect impact data (6 months)
3. Publish findings with psychology department
4. Expand to 10 universities
5. National rollout

### Metrics to Track

- Student registration rate
- Daily active users
- Assessment completion rate
- Counsellor booking rate
- Crisis detection accuracy
- Student satisfaction score
- Time to counsellor (metric: reduced wait time)
